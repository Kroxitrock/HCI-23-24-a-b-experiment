Human-Computer Interaction experiment

Hello, and thank you for participating in this experiment. It is conducted for the purposes of the "Evaluation of Interactive Systems" course which is part of the Human-Computer Interaction and Design program at Universidad Polit√©cnica de Madrid.

The experiment that you would be taking part of consists of 6 stages. In each stage you would be asked to select an item from a list of 50 items. After you successfully selected the item, another one would be randomly assigned to you. For half of the stages, the size of the objects will be bigger and for the rest it will be smaller. The order in which the scale is changed will be random. Please conduct the experiment only on a PC or Laptop and don't use the keyboard during the experiment.

After you conlcude the experiment, you will be redirected to a page on which you will be able to download the results of the experiment. We would ask you to send us those results. In the results there is no data about you as a person, and there is no way to identify you from the data. The parameters we collect are your speed and number of errors, so we would ask you to complete the experiment as effectively as possible.
